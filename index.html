How do WhatsApp messages work?

We integrate with WhatsApp to automatically send: order confirmations, delivery updates, birthday greetings, payment reminders, and promotional messages.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! There's no long-term contract. You can cancel your monthly subscription anytime.

Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

Not at all! Cherry is designed for bakery owners and staff. We provide complete training and the interface is intuitive. You'll be up and running in less than a day.

What about data security?

Your data is secured with enterprise-grade encryption, regular backups, and secure cloud hosting. We never share your customer data with third parties.
